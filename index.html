Get to know me

I'm a marketing and communications professional who's passionate about using her ever growing knowledge and expertise to impact businesses and people.

I allowed myself to explore opportunities in both public and private sectors within different industries all of which added a great deal of value to developing me to who I am today professionally & personally.

I find real value in communicating and connecting with people. That's why I dedicate part of my work towards personal branding where I help people realize their passion and communicate it effectively to the world.

I believe that one's quest in life shall be to achieve the highest possible levels of success while giving back and lifting others up. It's the main driver for me to teach AUC students about my passion and found the Sarah Seif organization.
